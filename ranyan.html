Our Work Process

We make it our mission to leverage our expertise to your advantage, proactively anticipating both building and tenant needs to eliminate performance gaps, mitigate risk, and redefine what you can expect from your property maintenance vendors




01.

INSPECT & PLAN

Perform site walk and inspection of entire facility. Conduct tenant interviews to identify special requirements. Enter all data into Servi-Trak.




02.

EQUIP & TRAIN

Procurement of uniforms, equipment, and supplies. Review and train staff on customized maintenance plans.




03.

DELIVER & INSPECT

Senior management on-site during the start-up phase to provide guidance and continued training. QA inspections begin. Reports generated.
